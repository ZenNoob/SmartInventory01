# **App Name**: Smart Inventory

## Core Features:

- Product & Category Management: Add, edit, and manage product categories and individual products with details like name, description, and cost.
- Sales Management: Create and manage sales transactions, including customer selection, product selection, quantity, price adjustments (for agents), and invoice printing.
- Debt Management: Track overall customer debt, record payment history, and manage individual customer credit limits.
- Reporting Dashboard: Generate sales reports, inventory reports, and debt reports with customizable date ranges and filters.
- Debt Risk Prediction: Utilize AI to predict the risk of non-payment by customers based on their payment history, credit limit, and other factors. This tool uses the customer information to assess risks.
- Inventory Shortage Prediction: Use AI to forecast potential product shortages based on historical sales data, current inventory levels, and upcoming sales events. This tool uses historical data and event data to forecast shortages.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Blue (#3F51B5) to convey trust and stability.
- Background color: Light gray (#F0F2F5) for a clean and professional look.
- Accent color: Orange (#FF9800) for key actions and highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, provides a modern and accessible feel.
- Use clear, simple icons to represent different product categories, sales actions, and reporting options.
- Responsive layout that adapts seamlessly to both desktop and mobile devices, ensuring a consistent user experience.
- Subtle animations for transitions and loading states to improve user engagement.
